Important Farming Tips by Season

Kharif Season (Jun-Oct)

• Ensure proper drainage to prevent waterlogging
• Use water-resistant varieties for flood-prone areas
• Monitor for pest outbreaks during humid conditions
• Apply nitrogen in split doses for better uptake

Rabi Season (Nov-Mar)

• Timely irrigation is critical for yield
• Protect from frost during Dec-Jan with mulching
• Always use certified seeds for disease resistance
• Apply phosphorus at sowing for root development

Summer Season (Apr-May)

• Focus on irrigation management to conserve water
• Grow short-duration crops to beat the heat
• Prepare land for upcoming monsoon season
• Maintain farm equipment during off-season

General Recommendations

• Test soil every 2 years for nutrient management
• Practice crop rotation to maintain soil health
• Use organic manure alongside chemical fertilizers
• Follow IPM practices for sustainable pest control

Government Schemes & Support

PM-KISAN

₹6,000 per year income support in 3 equal installments directly to farmer accounts. Eligibility: All landholding farmer families.

PMFBY (Crop Insurance)

Crop insurance at just 2% premium for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops. Covers yield losses due to natural calamities.

PMKSY (Irrigation)

Subsidy up to 55% for drip and sprinkler irrigation systems. Promotes water-use efficiency and higher productivity.

Soil Health Card

Free soil testing and personalized nutrient recommendations. Get your card from nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra.
